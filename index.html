What We Do

Have you always wanted to be apart of a band like Nirvana? Have you wanted to find people with your style to create a music group? Have you been looking for a certain asset to add to your group or band?

We Have Too.

This is the idea that birthed BandMi, a interface created to allow people from around the world to find each other and create music to their hearts content. BandMi gives user's the ability to search and audition for bands and create their music online using our interface to allow seamless collaboration without losing the interpersonal essence of a band.
