After the Storm.

A journey through the storms of being a young-adult.

There will always be storms in life.

That doesn't mean you have to go through them alone.

Recent studies have shown that the percentage of U.S. teens and young adults reporting mental distress, depression and suicidal thoughts and actions have risen significantly over the past decade

Your Storm is real

We hear you.

You are struggling. We are listening. After the Storm wants to help shift the tide in favor of mental stabilty. We want to connect you with real people who have been through similar seasons in their lives that may help guide you through your storms.

Our mission

We believe everyone has value

No matter what your past is, your present is, or your "percieved" future is, we believe that you have an intrinsic value that cannot be changed or altered. Our hope is that you leave feeling less anxious, less worried, and less depressed than you did before joining us.

How to join

We'd love to help you.

After the Storm is currently just an idea at the moment, but we would still love to hear from you whether you are looking for help through a storm, or to help those in need of a helping hand. Consinder becoming apart of our email list to stay updated and receive the occasional positive message.
